Privacy Policy — Time Sensei

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Time Sensei ("the App") is developed and operated by Kemal Mertcan Sağun ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the App collects, why, and how it's handled.

Information We Collect

  • Account identity. Every device is assigned an anonymous identity on first launch so scores can be submitted securely. If you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the identifier, name, and email address Apple/Google share with us for that sign-in.
  • Gameplay data. Scores, streaks, completed rounds, and leaderboard rankings, tied to your account identity.
  • Purchase records. If you buy Premium, the product purchased and a transaction identifier are stored to verify and restore your purchase — we never see or store your payment details; those are handled entirely by Apple/Google.
  • Crash and diagnostic data. If the App crashes or errors, technical details (device type, OS version, stack trace) may be sent to our crash-reporting provider to help us fix the issue.
  • Advertising data. If you choose to watch a rewarded ad, our advertising provider may collect an advertising identifier to serve and measure that ad, subject to your device's ad-tracking permission.

How We Use This Information

Solely to operate the App: authenticate you, save your progress, run the leaderboard, verify purchases, fix bugs, and (only where you opt in) show rewarded ads. We do not sell your data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following infrastructure providers, each processing data under their own privacy terms:

  • Supabase (database, authentication)
  • Apple (Sign in with Apple, App Store purchases)
  • Google (Google Sign-In; AdMob, if you watch a rewarded ad)
  • Sentry (crash reporting)

Data Retention & Deletion

Your gameplay data is retained as long as your account is active. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
