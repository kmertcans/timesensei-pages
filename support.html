Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Open Settings inside the app and tap "Restore Purchase" — Premium unlocks automatically if you've already bought it with the same Apple/Google account.

My streak reset — why?

Streaks require completing that day's 5 rounds before the day rolls over. Missing a full day resets it, unless you had a Streak Freeze banked (earned by clearing all 3 daily quests).
